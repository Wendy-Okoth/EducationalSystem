Our Institution

About Kasarani Academy

A premier high school in Nairobi, Kenya dedicated to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education.

Our Motto

"Strive for Excellence"

This motto embodies our commitment to pushing beyond limits, setting high standards, and encouraging every student to reach their fullest potential in academics, character, and personal development.

Our Vision

To be the leading high school in Kenya that produces globally competitive, ethically grounded, and socially responsible citizens through innovative education and holistic development.

"Shaping future leaders who excel academically and contribute positively to society."

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, moral values, and practical skills, preparing students for higher education and successful careers while fostering patriotism and social responsibility.

  • Academic Excellence
  • Character Development
  • Digital Literacy
